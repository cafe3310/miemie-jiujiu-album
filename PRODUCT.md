# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Cat lovers, friends, and creators interested in cat photos and original character designing.

## Product Purpose
A personal diary showcasing real cat photos alongside generated illustrations of original characters jiujiu & miemie. It serves as a personal gallery and a blog documenting creative model experiments.

## Brand Personality
Minimalist. Clean high-contrast typography, ample whitespace, and minimal decoration.

## Anti-references
Generic corporate template. Avoid identical card grids, side-stripe borders, icons, and hero metrics.

## Design Principles
1. Content is Hero: Photos and illustrations should dominate the visual focus. Avoid visual noise or unnecessary decorations.
2. Typographic Focus: Rely on size, weight contrast, and generous whitespace for structure instead of UI boundaries.
3. Structured Quietness: Maintain a calm, editorial flow using thin borders, simple layouts, and silent interactions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
1. Ultra-low Friction: Keep all interactions purely static with zero animations to guarantee instant load times.
2. Contrast Compliance: Ensure readable text contrast in accordance with WCAG AA guidelines.
